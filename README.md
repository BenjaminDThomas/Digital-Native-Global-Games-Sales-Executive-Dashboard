# Digital Native — Global Games Sales Executive Dashboard

**Tool:** Tableau
**Timeline:** 16 Jun 2026 – 23 Jun 2026
**Role:** Data Analyst

## Overview
Engineered a ten-sheet Tableau workbook from a raw, unstructured Kaggle video game sales dataset to answer a defined set of executive business questions for Digital Native. Identified and excluded incomplete launch-year records prior to modelling, preventing missing data points from distorting the time-series trend and forecast outputs, and applied the client's brand system (lime green, dark brown, neutral grey) across every visualisation for stakeholder-ready consistency.

## Analytical deliverables
- Modelled total global sales by launch year across 35,682M units of historical data to surface a peak-and-decline trend
- Ranked platform performance, identifying the PlayStation 2 as the highest revenue-generating platform
- Calculated regional sales as a percentage of total volume across four geographic markets, confirming the Americas as the dominant region at 49%
- Tracked genre release counts over time to evidence the Action, Sports, and Shooter genres' growth
- Applied Tableau's time-series forecasting model to project next year's global sales
- Ranked genre and platform revenue to identify Action as the optimal genre for future development
- Consolidated all findings into a single KPI-led executive dashboard
